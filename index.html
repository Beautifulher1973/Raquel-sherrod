Exposure Management Data Domain

A modern, scalable architecture delivering location-centric intelligence for underwriting, pricing, and analytics across global insurance operations.

$1B+ Opportunity Unlocked

🔍 The Problem

❌ Siloed, duplicate data and inconsistent formats
⏳ Manual, delayed underwriting and pricing updates
📊 Limited analytical capabilities due to poor data quality
⚙️ High IT costs from legacy tech stack

🌐 The Solution

Domain-driven design with a globally unique Building ID (BID), real-time data ingestion, master data governance, and GPU-accelerated analytics via Kinetica.

✅ Unified "Single Source of Truth"
🚀 Location-centric ingestion with GeoHash + fuzzy matching
📡 Real-time ingestion and enrichment pipelines
🧠 Self-service analytics (CloudForge)

📊 Kinetica Visual Analytics

Real-time rendering of claims, exposures, underwriting tiers, and peril overlays across millions of records—powered by GPU.
